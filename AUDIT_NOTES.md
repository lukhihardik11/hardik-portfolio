### 2. Light Mode Visibility
- Cards are nearly invisible in light mode - too transparent
- Glass pills and badges barely visible

### 3. Jelly OFF State (Default)

### 4. Jelly ON State

- Metaball cursor needs touch device fallback

### 5. Navbar
- "LIGHT" text label takes space, could be cleaner
- Jelly mode toggle icon is small and unclear

### 6. Engineering Section
- Scroll-to-disassemble works well with dark bg
- Component labels look good

### 7. General
- Section padding is excessive (py-20 sm:py-32 lg:py-40)
- Too much vertical space between sections
